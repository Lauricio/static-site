|| 10 || Enhancing the attendee experience

EventSpark enables the attendees to be more engaged in the event, and for you to provide them with a more personalised experience by providing them with…

A personalised agenda

Providing attendees with their own personal agenda’s has never been easier: whether you are looking for them to build their own (and consequently manage room allocation according to demand) or divide them into select groups with unique agendas, EventSpark gives you full control.

An interactive map

Attendees can now easily navigate their way to and within the venue, with an interactive map that is linked to the full agenda.

Their personal note taking feature

Attendees can compile and email their own notes to themselves from the app, mitigating the need to carry around pen and paper.
